Our Conservation Mission

The Ndakaini Dam provides over 80% of Nairobi's water supply. This marathon directly supports conservation efforts to protect this vital resource for current and future generations.

Every registration contributes to tree planting initiatives in the Aberdare watershed, helping to maintain the natural water cycle and prevent soil erosion that silts our reservoirs.

Your participation helps fund community education programs on water conservation, sustainable farming practices, and alternative energy sources to reduce pressure on our forests.

"For every kilometer you run, we pledge to protect 1,000 liters of clean water through our conservation programs."
