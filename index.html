Our Key Programs

Sustainability Village

Establishing a hub that repurposes plastic waste into useful products while promoting circular economy practices - championing Repurposing, Recycling and are Using. The Mazingira Sustainability village is inspired by kitengela Glasses and Sanaa Art Centre In Arusha Tanzania.

Environmental & Water Stewardship

Championing ecological stewardship through our community center. We implement proactive sustainability practices and initiatives to protect the bulk water system's ecosystems.

We'll also work with advocacy partners to ensure deliberate progresssive policy other will protect the future of the system

Sports & Agro Tourism for Conservation

We use events like the Ndakaini Mazingira Run to raise awareness and rally communities around environmental causes through sport talent pipelines and fitness.

Through partnership with other player we shall also roll out a eco tourism programme that will feature both farm tours as well as industrial tourism

  • Leg 3: Gatanga → Ndakaini 20 km
