Our Conservation Mission

The Ndakaini Dam watershed provides over 85% of Nairobi's water supply. Our centre leads community-driven initiatives to protect this vital resource through education, reforestation, and sustainable development.

We've planted over 2 million trees in the Aberdare ecosystem, maintaining the natural water cycle and preventing soil erosion that silts our reservoirs.

Our programs train local communities in water conservation, sustainable farming, and alternative energy to reduce pressure on forests.

"Every initiative at our centre contributes directly to safeguarding Nairobi's water security for future generations."

Getting to Ndakaini Dam

Route Breakdown

  • Leg 1: Nairobi → Thika (Blue Posts Hotel) 45 km
  • Leg 2: Thika → Gatanga Centre 16 km
  • Leg 3: Gatanga → Ndakaini 13 km
  • Total Distance: 74 km

Estimated drive time: 2-2.5 hours

Travel Tips:

  • All roads are tarmacked
  • Best departure time: 6:00-7:00 AM
  • Last fuel station at Thika
